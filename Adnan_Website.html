Muhammad Adnan Hanif is a structural engineer and lecturer with over a decade of experience spanning mega-infrastructure projects, sustainable-materials research, and university teaching. His work blends computational structural analysis with experimental testing and student mentorship, using tools such as OpenSees, MATLAB, SPSS, and Design Expert, and he is currently extending his research into machine-learning models. He is precise, adaptable, and focused on advancing sustainable construction in structural engineering.
